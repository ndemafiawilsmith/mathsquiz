How to Play

❓

10 Questions

Each game has 10 random maths questions using +, −, ×, ÷ and numbers 1–12.

⏱️

Timer (Medium & Hard)

Medium: 20 seconds per question. Hard: 10 seconds. Easy has no timer.

🔢

Type Your Answer

Use the number pad to type your answer, then press Submit. Use ⌫ to delete a digit.

✅

Scoring

1 point for every correct answer. Your score is shown throughout the game.

🔊

Sounds

Applause plays for correct answers! Use the 🔊 button to mute at any time.
